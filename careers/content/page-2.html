Announcement: Volume-based Cart Rates

Starting June 1, 2011, your refuse rates will be changing, and you will be charged a volume-based rate. The new rates will be reflected in your August/September utility bill from the City. Prices are set based on the size and quantity of GRAY TRASH CARTS at your residence. Therefore, you must place your order with Waste Management regarding the size and quantity of carts you want serviced at your residence (valid until April 22, 2011). Your first gray trash cart is one cost. Additional gray trash carts are available at a reduced cost. There is NO ADDITIONAL CHARGE for recycling and greenwaste carts.

At Home Sharps Disposal

Back to Top

Waste Management offers MB Customers up to three free At-Home Sharps Safe Disposal Kits per year. Each container above three per resident shall cost no more than $27.50 per container. Customers can contact Waste Management customer service to order their kits, and a WM representative will deliver it to your door. Then, fill it with sharps, and mail it back using the pre-paid postage included in the kit. Please make sure to fill out the enclosed form in each kit
