Announcement: Volume-based Cart Rates

Starting June 1, 2011, your refuse rates will be changing, and you will be charged a volume-based rate. The new rates will be reflected in your August/September utility bill from the City. Prices are set based on the size and quantity of GRAY TRASH CARTS at your residence. Therefore, you must place your order with Waste Management regarding the size and quantity of carts you want serviced at your residence (valid until April 22, 2011). Your first gray trash cart is one cost. Additional gray trash carts are available at a reduced cost. There is NO ADDITIONAL CHARGE for recycling and greenwaste carts.

Multi-Family Recycling Outreach Program

Back to Top

All cities in California are required to divert at least 50% of their waste from landfills and into recycling programs.  Recycling in single-family residents is on track to meet this goal, but the average multi-family complex only diverts between 3%-5%!  Waste Management’s Multi-Family recycling program is an easy way to get your building on track, and increase diversion and sustainability in Manhattan Beach – and best of all, it is FREE!  Waste Management will conduct a site visit of your complex, and help determine a specialized program customized for your building, including placement of containers, instructional flyers and posters, and individual apartment recyclers.  Tenants, ask your property manager to get in touch with WM customer service to sign up forc multi-family recycling. Property managers, what are you waiting for!

WM Shares

Back to Top

We all know that one man's trash is another man's treasure, but now Waste Management is making it easier than ever to connect with the right treasure-seekers in your community. WM SHARES is a simple, convenient, web-based re-use/ donation solution that lets you donate your unneeded items directly to local charities and schools. Waste Management is working with the highly regarded nonprofit LA Shares to bring this new program to Manhattan Beach.

The WM online database automatically matches donors and recipients to create a simple, seamless and immediate donation transaction that is completely web-based (URL coming soon).   To participate in the program, interested schools and nonprofits located in the City create an online profile of their organization, including a “Wish List” of items they need. The profile is entered into a database, which will proactively and continuously seek to match donation requests with items that others are seeking to donate Donated materials can be almost anything, including technology, office furniture/supplies, artwork, personal care items, and sports equipment. When matches are made, an email is sent to the recipient nonprofit/school to coordinate the donation transaction- the process is simple and seamless and can be completed in 24-72 hours. In addition, donors will receive a thank you note that they may use the donation for reporting purposes.

Coming this winter. Keep checking back for important updates regarding WM SHARES!
