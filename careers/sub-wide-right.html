Critical to success, employees' safety is our highest priority

One of Waste Management's strategic business goals is to be a "best place to work." In order to achieve that, we seek to develop an engaged workforce and a positive work environment. Safety is on the forefront of our effort to keep employees happier and more productive.

Waste Management has actively pursued it's "Mission to Zero ™" plan, to eventually reach zero accidents or injuries. Leaders have emerged from this program who have all helped to give classroom instruction, monitor safety data, and train drivers and other employees to be the safest in the industry. Some amazing improvements we have seen over the years:

  • Decrease of 85% between 2000 and 2009 in Total Recordable Injury Rate
  • 60% improvement in Vehicle accident rates
  • Over $600 million in investment in maintenance for vehicles and heavy equipment
  • 310,000 hours of training through "Waste Management University", our enterprise-wide learning and development program

All drivers go through 80 hours of training in and out of the classroom, and regular evaluations ensure they are on the right track. One of only 8 companies to partner with OSHA to participate in its invitation-only Voluntary Protection Program, Waste Management is on its way to receiving Star Certifications at facilities around the country.

Safety is essential at Waste Management, for our employees, for our customers, and for the success of our business. If you're ready to make a commitment to safety, start your application process today!
